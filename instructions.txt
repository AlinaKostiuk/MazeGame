
*** Instructions ***
- Type <direction> <number of tiles> to move up, down, left or right.
  For example, 'left 2' means you'll move two tiles to the left.
  If you don't type a number, you'll move 1 tile.
- Type 'map' to see the maze with your currect location.
- Type 'help' to see the instructions again.
- Type 'exit' to give up.
